# Car Sales Data Analysis using Power BI
## Project Objective
The objective of this project is to build an interactive Car Sales Dashboard in Power BI to analyze YTD sales performance, track key KPIs like total sales, average price, and cars sold, and provide insights into trends, regional performance, and company-wise growth to support data-driven business decisions. The project also demonstrates the use of data cleaning, data modeling, DAX, and time intelligence functions to create a real-world business intelligence solution.

## Dataset Used
- <a href= "https://github.com/DeekshithReddy03/Car-Sales-Data-Analysis-using-Power-BI/blob/main/Car%20Sales.xlsx">Dataset</a>

## Questions (KPIs)
- YTD Total Sales
- MTD Total Sales
- YTD Average Selling Price
- MTD Average Selling Price
- YTD Cars Sold
- MTD Cars Sold
- Year-over-Year (YOY) Sales Growth %
- Sales Difference (YTD vs PYTD)
- Weekly YTD Sales Trend
- Company-wise YTD Sales
- Sales by Body Style
- Sales by Dealer Region
- Sales by Customer Gender

## Process
- Understood the business requirements and defined important KPIs to measure sales performance and growth.
- Imported the Car Sales dataset into Power BI and verified all data types and fields.
- Cleaned and transformed the data using Power Query to ensure accuracy and consistency.
- Created a dedicated Date Table and built proper relationships to establish an efficient data model.
- Developed DAX measures to calculate Total Sales, YTD, MTD, Previous Year Sales, YOY Growth %, Average Selling Price, and Cars Sold.
- Designed KPI cards to clearly display core business metrics at a glance.
- Built the main dashboard with trend charts, distribution visuals, maps, and company-wise performance analysis.
- Created a detailed report page to display transaction-level sales data with proper formatting.
- Implemented slicers and cross-filtering to make the dashboard fully interactive and dynamic.
- Validated all calculations, optimized the layout, and finalized the dashboard for presentation and sharing.

## Project Insights
- The business shows steady Year-to-Date (YTD) sales growth, indicating consistent market demand throughout the year
- Certain weeks recorded peak sales performance, highlighting seasonal or promotional impact on revenue generation.
- SUVs and higher-end body styles contribute significantly to total revenue, showing strong customer preference for premium segments.
- The average selling price remains stable, suggesting consistent pricing strategy without major fluctuations.
- Some dealer regions outperform others, indicating geographical concentration of sales performance.
- Company-wise analysis reveals that a few brands dominate total sales, while others contribute moderately.
- Year-over-Year (YOY) growth percentage reflects positive business expansion compared to the previous year.

## Final Conclusion
The Car Sales Dashboard project successfully transforms raw transactional data into meaningful business insights through effective data cleaning, modelling, and DAX-driven analysis. By tracking YTD performance, sales trends, regional distribution, and company-wise growth, the dashboard provides a clear view of revenue performance and business expansion. Overall, the project demonstrates the practical application of Power BI and business intelligence concepts to support data-driven decision-making in a real-world sales environment.
